Custom Clearance

We’re one of the leading Customs Clearing Agents in Mumbai & Pune, ensuring smooth and easy customs clearance for all our customers, so that they receive their goods on time. Our Customs Brokers help ease Import and Export regulations and paperwork in record time for all of your shipments. Handling all the trade compliance and procedures, we help clear consignments by sea and air more efficiently. Over the years, we have cleared all types of shipments, both major and minor, for a variety of goods from across the world with utmost precision. Being leading customs clearing agents our expert team do a proper study of all local rules and regulations so that they can help you overcome even the most complex matters of trade compliance. We identify the demands, challenges and hurdles for your business and help you arrange your paper work accordingly so that day-to-day formalities are met and business flows smoothly.
Our in-depth knowledge and understanding of this sector has helped us offer customized solutions to all of our clients so that they can reduce lead time, optimize cash flow and minimize customs duties. With the required documents, our team of dedicated professionals ensures end-to-end solutions for both Import Customs Clearance and Export Customs Clearance in the most cost-effective manner. We have an in-depth understanding of stringent regulation formalities and a well-managed documentation process coupled with competent, efficient professional man-power focuses on personalized monitoring and smoother clearance of shipments.

Export Custom Clearance: Preparation of shipping bill (on the basis of shipper's letter of instruction), classification for drawback, DEPB or DEEC receipt of goods, measurement, weighing, marking, labeling processing custom clearance, custom examination and obtaining custom out charge and Handing over to international Career.
Import Custom Clearance: HSN classification, duty determination, preparation of bill of entry, processing the custom clearance, assessment, custom examination and obtaining custom out of charge and arranging dispatch, delivery to the destination.

FUMIGATION

Fumigation is a legal requirement by the buyer in most of the countries. So, fumigation certificate is issued by the fumigator by obtaining approval for fumigation from the licensing authority. Most of the countries will not allow importing goods without fumigation certificate, wherever applicable on such goods. We provide dedicated agencies that are efficiently do this process with required legal documentation process.

We Provide Fumigation For
• General purpose or Dry Freight (for general cargo commodities)
• Insulated or thermal (for chilled or frozen foods)
• Fantainers (for products requiring ventilation )
• Flat rack or platforms (for heavy machinery)
• Open top and bulk (for OD cargo)
• Tank (for liquids)
